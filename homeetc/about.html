ABOUT EduHub

EduHub is a web-based learning platform designed to make education accessible, engaging, and effective for students and educators alike. Our platform supports interactive learning, assessment tracking, and personalized progress monitoring, ensuring that quality education reaches every student, even in resource-limited environments.

Developed by a passionate group of five students from Uva Wellassa University, EduHub is built with a focus on innovation, accessibility, and quality education.


Join EduHub today and take your learning journey to the next level !
